## Pricing

All voices cost the same:
- **tts-1**: $15 per 1 million characters
- **tts-1-hd**: $30 per 1 million characters (higher quality)

## Voice Selection

The system automatically:
- **Narrator**: Uses your selected voice preference
- **Characters**: Assigns voices based on:
  - Gender
  - Age
  - Personality traits

All 13 voices are available for character dialogue, giving you great variety for multi-character books!
